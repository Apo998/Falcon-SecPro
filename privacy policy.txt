Falcon GmbH (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides the Falcon Security Mobile App for operational use by authorized security workers. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, and protected.

1. Who this app is for
This app is for employees and authorized personnel of [Company Name]. It is not intended for public consumer use.

Depending on your role and use of the app, we may collect:

Account and profile data (name, email, role, employee identifier)
Shift and work activity data (assigned shifts, accepted shifts, attendance/check-in/check-out)
Location data (for shift verification, patrol, SOS, and operational logging)
Incident and report data (text, photos, attachments, timestamps)
Communication data (group chat messages, broadcast interactions)
Device/app data (device type, app version, log/error information)
Notification data (push token, notification status)

We process data to:

Manage workforce scheduling and open shift assignment
Support patrol workflows and operational reporting

Maintain security, audit trails, and fraud/misuse prevention
Comply with legal and contractual obligations

Compliance with legal obligations
Consent (where required, e.g., specific permissions)
